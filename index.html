How I Work

I’m currently enrolled in SEG3125 (User Interface Design) at the University of Ottawa, where I'm learning visual communication principles and UX design methodologies. My approach is influenced by the research and guidelines provided by the Nielsen Norman Group (NN/g), particularly their 10 Usability Heuristics for User Interface Design. These principles help me focus on creating intuitive, user-centered designs.
